What is temporary email or disposable email?

The temporary or disposable e-mail service is a free service that provides you with an e-mail address with the click of a button and does not require any personal information to get one. It provides you with the ability to register on different websites, services, and applications and then receive an activation email message or activation code for the created account without providing them with your personal e-mail address, thus maintaining your privacy and protecting your mailbox from being filled with spam promotional messages in the future. You may have heard of the service with names like temporary email, disposable email, temp email, virtual email, anonymous email, fake email, Mohmal, 10minute mail, Moakt, Gmailnator, Guerrilla Mail, a quick email, 10minutemail, temp-mail.

What is the need for temporary mail?

As you know that most Internet websites such as social networking sites such as Facebook, Twitter, TikTok, Instagram, and books, games, movies, blogs websites, etc., require an email to signup and enjoy the various services they provide you or even read the content and post a comment or download a document or a file. Previously, people used to use their personal email that they got from Gmail, Outlook/Hotmail, Zoho Mail, Mail, Mailbox, or similar services to register and receive the activation email message containing a link or code sent to verify the account. The problem is not here, but the consequences for you if you use your personal email, is that all of these services now have your email address and this may expose you to many annoying spam messages in the future. The solution is to keep your personal mail private and use temporary mail instead so that the important messages you receive from friends and relatives or even work will not be lost and mixed with hundreds of advertisements for makeup products, jewelry, watches, traveling, flights, hotel offers, etc.
